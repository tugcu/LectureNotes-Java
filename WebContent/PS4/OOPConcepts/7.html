University Registration System

Let's write a library to represent our university's registration system. The code will be incomplete, you can finish it at home.
  • Classes: Department, Course, Student
  • We must start with defining states and behaviors.
  • A department
    • has a number of students and courses, both of which have a fixed quota
    • can increase the quota (thanks to YOK)
  • A course
    • is offered by a particular department
    • has a number of students registered
    • may be selective or not
    • may require another course as a prerequisite
    • has a fixed credit
  • A student
    • has a major in a department
    • may have a double-major program
    • can take multiple courses
    • drop a course
    • <\br>
