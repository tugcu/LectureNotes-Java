Static Method vs. Instance Method

  • You used only static methods in CmpE150.
    • E.g.: Math.pow(2,3.0);
      • Note that Math is a class. You invoke the method pow through a class.
  • In the case of an instance method, you invoke the method through an object, i.e. an instance.
    • E.g.: circle1.getArea();
      • Note that getArea is called in the context of object circle1. Therefore, when it accesses radius, we know it is the radius of circle1.
