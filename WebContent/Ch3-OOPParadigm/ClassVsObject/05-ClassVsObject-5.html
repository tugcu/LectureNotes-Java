Revisiting the Transportation Example

  • Each of Automobile, Bus, FourWheels, EnginePowered, etc. is a class.
    • However, vehicles with plate ids 34MT475, 34AB579, 54KY128, and 24RG315 are all specific instances of the Motorcycle and Automobile classes, respectively.
      • You implement each class only once, and then create multiple instances out of (some of) them.
      • 34AB579, 54KY128, and 24RG315 are objects of the same class but they are separate entities. They have common features (each automobile has an engine, driver, etc.) as well as different ones (they belong to different people, have traveled different distances, etc.)
