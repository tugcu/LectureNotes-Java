Example Scenario (via Structured Procedural Programming)

  • Let's assume we want to simulate the traffic in Istanbul.
  • There are more than 3.3 million vehicles (as of Dec 2014). We have many automobiles, buses, trucks, etc. to simulate.
  • If we use the structured procedural programming approach:
    • We need an array of vehicles.
    • We should continuously traverse the array to update the coordinates of all vehicles.
    • We should make sure that their coordinates do not overlap (i.e., they don't collide).
    • In the real traffic, the cars are autonomous objects that act according to the environment and by communicating with other vehicles (by signaling, visual contact, etc.)
    • Thus,the way we program is in fact quite different than the real world.
