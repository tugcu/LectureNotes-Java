Complexity of Towers of Hanoi

  • Let $$T_N$$ be the minimum number of moves needed to solve the puzzle with N disks
  • Move twice (N - 1) disks from one peg to another and making one additional move in between
    $$T_N = T_{N-1}+1 = 2 \cdot T_{N-1}+1 \quad where \quad T_0=0$$
  • Define $$S_i=T_i+1 (s.t. S_0=1, S_N=2 \cdot S_{N-1})$$
