Shape Area and Perimeter

  • Rectangle (as defined by width w and height h):
    • area = w · h
    • perimeter = 2·w + 2·h
  • Circle (as defined by radius r):
    • area = π · r2
    • perimeter = 2·π·r
  • Triangle (as defined by side lengths a, b, and c)
    • area = (s·(s-a)·(s-b)·(s-c))    (Heron's formula)
      • where s = a+b+c 2
    • perimeter = a+b+c
