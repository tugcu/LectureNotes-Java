AVL Trees

  • Observation: BSTs that are balanced will have \(log \ n\) search time for contains.
    • It is desirable to have a BST that stays balanced
  • AVL tree: A binary search tree that uses modified add and remove operations to stay balanced as items are added into it
    • Invented in 1960 by two Russian mathematicians:
      • Georgii Maksimovich Adelson-Velskii
      • Evgenii Mikhailovich Landis
    • One of several auto-balancing trees
