Nodes and Levels in a Complete Binary Tree

  • Number of the nodes in a tree with M levels: $$ 1 + 2 + 2^2 + â€¦. 2^M = 2^{M+1} - 1 = 2*2^M - 1$$
  • Let N be the number of the nodes.
    • $$ N = 2*2^M - 1, \qquad 2*2^M = N + 1 $$ $$ 2^M = (N+1)/2 $$ $$ M = log((N+1)/2) $$
  • N nodes: $$ log( (N+1)/2 ) = O(log(N)) \quad levels $$
  • M levels: $$ 2^{M+1} - 1 = O(2^M) \quad nodes$$
