Empirical analysis

  • Beware that empiric analysis is quite subjective and debatable
    • The scenario(s) under which you test the competing programs may favor one over the other
    • Your scenario must be openly declared
      • and you must have multiple and reasonable scenarios so that one of the competitors does not abuse your benchmark test
    • Beware of code tuning
      • If performance ratio of the two competitors is constant (independent of the input size), probably the better one has done parameter tuning
