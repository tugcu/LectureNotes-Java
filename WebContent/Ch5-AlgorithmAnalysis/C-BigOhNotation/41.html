Comparing functions

  • same rate of growth: g(n) = Θ(f(n))
  • different rate of growth:
    • either g(n) = o (f(n))
            g(n) grows slower than f(n),
            and hence f(n) = ω(g(n))
    • or g(n) = ω (f(n))
            g(n) grows faster than f(n),
            and hence f(n) = o(g(n))
