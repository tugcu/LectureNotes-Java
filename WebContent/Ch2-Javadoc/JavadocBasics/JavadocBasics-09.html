HTML in Javadoc comments

  • Doc comments can be written in HTML
  • You must replace
    • ”<” with ”&lt;”
    • ”>” with ”&gt;”
    • “&” with “&amp;”
  • Other things you may use:
    • <i>...</i> for italics
    • <b>...</b> for boldface
  • Avoid <h1> <h2>
  • Inside the comment block, use <p> to separate paragraphs and javadoc predefined tags to define specific elements
